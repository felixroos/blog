
### Optimizations

Some future optimizations may include:

- beam support: `a4 b4 . _b4` or `a4 b4 . _` to replace hacky usage of length: `a4 b4_3 . r`
  - this would also fit staff notation convention
- Ignore spaces (currently, extra spaces will create a rest) => for pretty printing
- Multiline support + be able to add extra "|" after newline
- full string format support

With all those goodies, it could look like this:

```
@bpm 200
| g5  . d5 b4 . f#5   . d5 b4  | e5 f#5   . c#5 e5 . d#5 c5 . a4 b4    |
| _b4 . g4 e4 . a4 b4 . g4 f#4 | f4 a4 c5 . e5 eb5 . r g4   . g4 a4 g4 |
```
